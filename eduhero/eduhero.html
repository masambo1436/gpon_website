What is the EduHero Challenge?

The EduHero Challenge is a monthly competition that focuses on raising awareness about Education in Emergencies (EiE). Share stories, solutions, or create campaigns to inspire change.



EduHero Concept!

#EduHeroChallenge will be a monthly developmental challenge designed to engage individuals, communities, and organizations on the topic of Education in Emergencies.

It aims to spark creativity, raise awareness, and inspire solutions to the challenges children face in accessing quality education during crises.

Each month, participants will respond to a specific education-related challenge or task for a chance to win the star prize of ₦500,000

PARTICIPATE!


Participation Criteria:

  1. Open to individuals and groups in Nigeria (and optionally worldwide).
  2. Entries must align with the monthly theme and meet submission requirements.


How To Participate!

  • Create and a short video (1-2 minutes) proposing a practical to inproving education access or learning outcomes in line with the Theme for the month.
  • Share a personal story or case study about EiE impact.
  • Create an infographic advocating for education during crises.
